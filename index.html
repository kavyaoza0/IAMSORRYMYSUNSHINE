From me to you

My Sunshine,

As I sit here writing this, sorrow wraps itself around me, knowing I have been the cause of your pain. The pain I have caused, is a reflection of my mistake, and I'm truly sorry for causing you any sadness. You deserve nothing but happiness, and I regret that my actions have dimmed the light in your beautiful smile.

Since you walked into my life, everything has changed in ways I could have never imagined. You've shown me a kindness so rare and a trust so sacred that it humbles me every time I think about it. Like the seasons that quietly shape the earth, your presence has reshaped my world, making it richer, softer, and more alive. I know that I have hurt you, and it tears me apart knowing that I let my actions cast even the smallest shadow over your light. You deserve someone who sees the incredible person you are. I promise to be more aware, more patient, and more deserving of the gift that is you. No matter how many words I write, none could ever fully express the depth of my gratitude for you and the fierce desire I have to make things right.

Please forgive me. I promise to learn from this, to grow, and to be the partner you deserve.

Forever yours,
Kavya
